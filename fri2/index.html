The future of identification
An identity document is a document that can be used to prove a person's identity. An identity document is used to link a person to information
about that person, usually in a database. A photograph and the person who owns it are used to associate that person with the document.
The link between the identity document and the information database is based on the personal information on the document. With the
development of technology, people are not only using a single ID card to identify themselves, increasingly convenient and simple
ways are emerging
